Faye Heller was born in Cornwall in 1972. She slowly made her way up the country (stopping at Plymouth and Farnham) to London, over to Amsterdam, then Brighton where she currently lives with her three children, partner and dog.

She studied her MA in Fine Art at The Slade School, University College London.

She exhibits in London, Amsterdam, and Italy (email for further details).

As well as making art she has spent a considerable time working in a second-hand bookshop, then, after completing a teaching postgraduate qualification she worked as a special needs teacher in a London school.

She now lives close to her main love of her life – the sea.
